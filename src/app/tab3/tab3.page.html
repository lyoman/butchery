About Us

Butchery locator is an online platform that helps farmers to locate the nearest butcheries and sell their livestock.

It shows the locations of nearest butcheries in your area and also the contact details of the butcheries.

Butcheries can login into the platform and create order requests for what they are looking for. E.g a butchery can request that they want 50kg of beef, or pork or 100 chickens, in return the farmers will contact the butcheries.
